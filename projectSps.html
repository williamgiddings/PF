Introduction

Untitled Space Game was my first year project at Lincoln University as part of out Games Programming module. This was designed and created in 4 months

The game itself was a wave/level based space shooter where the player must fight against waves of enemies in increasing difficulties leading up to a boss fight at the end of the game.

Features include:

  • Random fleet formations based on difficulty.
  • A variety of enemies each with different weapons and stats
  • 4 Different power-up types
  • High Score system
  • Visual ship damage effects
  • Fly through the wormholes to progress to the next level

Tools used:

  • Unity Engine
  • C#
  • Github
  • Email
  • Play Now!
